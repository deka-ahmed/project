THE UNIVERSE

The universe is a boundless expanse of stars, planets, and cosmic wonders. It holds the echoes of ancient light and the birthplaces of galaxies. In its silence, it reminds us how small we are—and how infinite the possibilities.

GLAXY

A galaxy is a glowing city of stars, dust, and dark matter, all bound together by gravity. Ours—called the Milky Way—spirals quietly through space, carrying countless worlds within. Each galaxy is a chapter in the story of the universe, written in light.

Solar System

The solar system is our cosmic neighborhood, where the Sun reigns at the center. Around it, eight planets orbit in silent harmony, each with its own story and mystery. From scorching Mercury to icy Neptune, it's a delicate dance of light, gravity, and time.

planets

Planets are worlds adrift in the light of their stars, each shaped by time, gravity, and chance. Some are rocky and warm, others icy and distant, wrapped in clouds or rings. Together, they remind us that the universe is not empty—but full of places waiting to be discovered.
